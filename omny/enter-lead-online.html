1. C.2 Make Enquiry (from website) [check circle o]

1.1. Actor: Internet User

1.2. User story

The user identifies (or is contacted by) an individual who may or may not be associated with an organisation and who is potentially interested in the product or service offered by the tenant. The user enters the lead into the Contact page.

1.3. User outcome

A new contact and, optionally, account recorded in the CRM with some arbitrary set of standard and custom fields filled out.

1.4. Implementation pre-requisites

  • Create tenancy, inc. ‘Bot’

  • Register client users

  • Enter Lead - offline

1.5. Implementation

  • Test form and check contact arrives correct in all details. Also that email is sent to enquirer and alert (if configured) to contact owner
